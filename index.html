About Me

My name is Amparo Davalos and I am a UX/UI designer and a front-end developer. I am currently on my last year at UC San Diego as a Cognitive Science major with specialization in Human-Computer Interaction. As a student, I have been able to work on a few different projects focusing on user-centered design and front-end development.

In the process, I have learned to value the ability of working as a team as much as the quality of the work. It's important to be able to communicate with your team and find different ways to express ideas.
